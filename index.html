About

Lil Gazzy is a rising star in the world of hip-hop and rap, known for his high-energy beats and catchy hooks that keep listeners coming back for more. With a unique blend of trap and pop influences, Lil Gazzy has been making waves in the music industry since his debut in 2022. Born and raised in the streets of Norwich, Lil Gazzy was inspired by the sounds of his town's vibrant music scene, as well as the struggles and triumphs of his own life. Through his music, Lil Gazzy tells his story and connects with fans on a deep and personal level. Lil Gazzy's debut single, "Wokanda'," quickly gained traction on social media and streaming platforms, earning him a loyal following of fans who appreciate his raw talent and authentic approach to music. Since then, Lil Gazzy has released a string of hit singles and collaborated with some of the biggest names in the game.
